The world of H&V is a modern cityscape world called [[WayHaven]].

It is a place of Quirks, Creatures, Weird individuals and even regular civilians in a spectacular adventure. It has all the elements required to reproduce the BNHA feel as well as endless adventure or misadventure only limited by your imagination.
