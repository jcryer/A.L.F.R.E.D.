Lyon Johannes, a.k.a. 'Orchestra', is a man of fine taste occupying a spot in the higher class inhabiting WayHaven. A man with a golden tongue, he persuades his listeners with ease, luring them into his web.


== Personality ==
A manipulative and charasmatic man, a calm control freak who always gets his way like a spoiled brat. Cunning with a flair for the dramatic, with a love of music.
== Backstory ==
<p style="font-weight:400;">Grew up with a rich family of music players and as the youngest sibling grew to learn that by acting in certain ways, people will do anything for you. As his skill with music grew so did his mischieviousness which continued over the years untill finally, when he was 18, he instigated the murders of his entire family, so that as the last remaining member of the Johannes bloodline he inhereted everyone they ever owned. Lyon Johannes always gets his way.</p>
