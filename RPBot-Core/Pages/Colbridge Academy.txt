== Located at Turner Street ==
Before the age of heroes, Colbridge was the first place the elite would think of sending their children to study and imbibe the laws of the world. Even now, it still retains its reputation as one of the most prestigious academies in the world with a student body of all sorts seeking to study everything but Heroics.
