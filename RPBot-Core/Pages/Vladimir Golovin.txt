Bartender and bouncer of the [[WayHaven|Matryoshka Troika]] restaurant (located in the Providence market district) and right hand man of the leader of the [[Volch'ya Staya]]. A stern, yet friendly face. He keeps his customers happy by keeping trouble out.

==Personality<nowiki/>==
Loyal and protective. Zealous for his cause, which is the cause of the family. The Staya means everything to him.

== Resources ==
Runs the bar at the '[[WayHaven|Matryoshka Troika]]' restaurant. Relatively well off. Has a good car and nice house.
