He went to the League of Villains, because he always had the urge to kill people, to see blood spilled, people killed and bodies lying on the floor, lifeless.

== Personality ==
He is usually patient, calm and quiet.

But when his bloodlust kicks in, he exhibits his crazy nature, and in the state he loves to kill everything standing in his way.

== Resources ==
Employed in the League of Villains by [[Nightmare]] as an A-rank, he gets a hefty salary, and while on a mission, as much is required to give desirable results.

== Abilities ==

=== '''Quirk''' ===
'''Iron Blade'''

Hayato can transform his limbs into different iron blades. 

The iron blades are as long as the limb.
