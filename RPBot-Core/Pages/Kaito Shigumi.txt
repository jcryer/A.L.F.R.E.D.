Bartender at the [[WayHaven|Booze 'n Bikes]] bar (located in the Providence market district) and right hand man of the [[En-Jin]] motor club leader. A calm man who has a very busy job, keeping in contact with the underworld of [[WayHaven]] and delivering contracts to the shady people of this city.



==Personality<nowiki/>==

Kind, but serious. Social. Doesn't stand 'outsiders', i.e. those that are not from the underworld.



== Resources ==

Bartender at the [[WayHaven|Booze 'n Bikes]] bar. Delivers contracts to the rogues and villains of [[WayHaven]]. Relatively well off. Has a good Thunder Mountain motorcycle and decent house.
